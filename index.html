Meet The Team

Audrey Ng has always been driven by a passion for making a positive impact on people’s lives. Her experience working with children—as a camp counselor and a volunteer for middle school sports—has deepened her commitment to ensuring every child has the best possible childhood. Within her own family, she has seen firsthand how chronic illnesses and health challenges, including cognitive and physical disabilities and cancer, affect families. These experiences have only strengthened her dedication to supporting them. Outside of this organization, Audrey enjoys science, athletics, and music.

Hasan Ramadan is a dedicated junior at Bloomfield Hills High School with a strong passion for giving back to his community. He actively seeks opportunities to create lasting benefits and meaningful experiences for those around him. Whether volunteering at religious events, organizing fundraisers to support new school projects, or helping clubs raise funds to participate in district tournaments, he is always looking for ways to make a positive impact. Hasan is excited to bring his enthusiasm and commitment to this organization, hoping to help children in his community experience joy and create cherished memories—something he believes is essential throughout life.

Simone Opdahl has always wanted and enjoyed helping people and making an impact in their lives. Her experience with children includes volunteering at a hospital, being a camp counselor, and working with middle schoolers on different sports and extracurriculars. Through these experiences, she has seen how illness and health challenges impact and affect families. This has only made her want to help even more and support them in any way she can. Outside of this organization, Simone enjoys spending time with her friends, science, and volunteering anywhere she can.
